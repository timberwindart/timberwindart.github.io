About Me

I'm a Washington-based digital artist with a strong interest in spaceflight, planetary science, infrastructure, and anthropomorphic critters. I've been a space enthusiast for approximately as long as I remember, and in recent years I've finally resolved to put something cohesive together out of the concepts I've always liked to throw around. I hope you enjoy what I make!
